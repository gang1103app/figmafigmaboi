## Troubleshooting

### Notifications Not Appearing?

**Check Browser Permission:**
1. Look for ðŸ”” icon in your browser's address bar
2. Make sure notifications are "Allowed"
3. If blocked, you need to change it in browser settings

**Chrome:**
- Click the ðŸ”” icon in address bar
- Or go to: Settings > Privacy and security > Site Settings > Notifications

**Firefox:**
- Click the ðŸ”” icon in address bar
- Or go to: Preferences > Privacy & Security > Permissions > Notifications

**Safari:**
- Safari > Preferences > Websites > Notifications

**Still not working?**
- Check if "Do Not Disturb" mode is on (macOS/Windows)
- Try in a different browser
- Clear cache and try again

### Daily Reminder Not Coming?

Daily reminders only trigger:
- After 6:00 PM
- If you haven't logged in that day
- Once per day maximum
- Only if toggle is ON in Settings

### Friend/Leaderboard Notifications?

These check periodically:
- Leaderboard: Every 5 minutes
- Friends: Every 10 minutes

So there may be a slight delay before you see the notification.
